# AI Image Generation Prompts for ChiChat

Use these prompts to generate consistent scene backgrounds and NPC portraits.
All images should share a cohesive art style — warm, inviting, slightly stylized illustration.

## Style Guide
- **Art style**: Semi-realistic digital illustration, warm color palette, soft lighting
- **Backgrounds**: 16:9 aspect ratio, 1920x1080px, slight depth-of-field blur
- **Portraits**: Square or 3:4 aspect ratio, transparent or solid-color background, waist-up composition
- **Mood**: Welcoming, friendly, approachable — this is a language learning app
